Look At Our Trips!

Rafting with us is an immersion into nature's pulse, an adrenaline-fueled dance with untamed waters, and a camaraderie forged through adventure. Our seasoned guides, with a passion for both the sport and the environment, ensure your safety while unlocking the thrill of every rapid. Whether you're a novice seeking an introduction or an experienced rafter craving the ultimate challenge, our meticulously curated trips cater to all

Mark "Danger Special" Hale, the intrepid founder of our rafting company, is a legend in the world of white-water adventure. With a spirit as untamed as the rapids he conquers, Mark has spent decades exploring some of the most challenging waterways on the planet. His expertise and passion for the sport have not only made him a sought-after guide but have also inspired a new generation of thrill-seekers. Mark's fearless approach to rafting, particularly on the "Danger Special" expeditions, has earned him respect and admiration throughout the industry. His commitment to safety and environmental conservation is the cornerstone of our company's ethos, ensuring every expedition is not only an adrenaline rush but also a responsible and unforgettable journey. Join us on a trip led by the legendary Mark Hale, and let the river reveal the heart-pounding adventure he's dedicated his life to sharing.
