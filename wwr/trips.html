Full Course

Embark on the ultimate adrenaline-pumping journey with our Wild Rapids Expedition, a full course rafting trip that promises an unforgettable adventure through the heart of untamed nature. Led by expert guides, you'll navigate a series of exhilarating Class III to Class V rapids, immersing yourself in the rugged beauty of some of the most breathtaking landscapes on Earth. Between the intense rapids, bask in serene moments of tranquility, camp under a canopy of stars, and forge lasting connections with fellow adventurers around the evening campfire. This trip is designed for both seasoned paddlers seeking an adrenaline rush and beginners eager to embrace a new, invigorating challenge, all with state-of-the-art equipment, top-notch safety measures, and a commitment to environmental stewardship. Join us on the Rapids Expedition and let the river lead you to a world of adventure!

Half Course

Dive into the heart-pounding excitement of our Rapid Rush half course rafting trip, a thrilling journey designed for those seeking a taste of the white-water adrenaline rush. Led by seasoned guides, you'll conquer a dynamic stretch of Class II and III rapids, offering an exhilarating yet accessible experience for adventurers of all levels. In just a few hours, you'll navigate through captivating scenery, taking in the natural wonders that surround you. This half course expedition provides an ideal introduction to the world of rafting, ensuring an unforgettable adventure packed with excitement and awe-inspiring beauty. Join us on the Rapid Rush and let the river unveil an unforgettable aquatic adventure!

Danger Special

Prepare for an adrenaline-charged odyssey on our Danger Special extreme rafting trip, an adventure tailored for the boldest thrill-seekers. Led by our elite team of expert guides, this expedition takes you through the heart-stopping Class IV and V rapids, providing an unparalleled white-water experience that will push your limits. This high-octane journey promises a rush of excitement as you navigate through turbulent waters, surrounded by awe-inspiring, untamed landscapes. With top-of-the-line equipment and rigorous safety protocols, we ensure an exhilarating yet secure adventure for those seeking the ultimate rafting challenge. The Danger Special is not for the faint of heart, but for those who crave the most intense, heart-pounding rush, it promises an unforgettable, once-in-a-lifetime ride. Join us on this extreme rafting expedition and let the river take you on the adventure of a lifetime!
